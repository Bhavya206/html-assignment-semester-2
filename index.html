Frequently Asked Questions

What is ‘Code Every Day with Vivek’?

It's a daily coding program designed to build consistency and improve problem-solving skills.

Who is this program for?

It's for everyone who wants to sharpen their coding and problem-solving skills —beginners, students, job seekers, and working professionals.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is necessary. We start from the basics and guide you through.

Which programming languages will be used?

We primarily use C++, Java, and Python for data structures and algorithms.

How does this work?

You'll receive daily problems, video solutions, and mentorship to keep you on track.

What happens on non-live days?

You can practice previously solved problems or work on bonus challenges.

How do I join the community?

Sign up and you'll get an invite link to our exclusive Discord community.

How do I invite people?

Use your unique referral link found in your dashboard to invite friends and earn karma.
